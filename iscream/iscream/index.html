Our Flavors

Pink Cherry Ice Cream

Ingredients: Cherry puree, cream, sugar, vanilla extract.

Preparation: Mix cherry puree with cream and sugar, churn in an ice cream maker, and freeze until firm.

Calories: 200 per serving

Our Pink Cherry Ice Cream is a delightful treat with a rich cherry flavor, perfect for a summer day.

Lavender Honey Ice Cream

Ingredients: Lavender, honey, cream, milk, sugar.

Preparation: Infuse lavender in cream, mix with honey and milk, churn, and freeze.

Calories: 180 per serving

Experience the calming blend of lavender and honey in this unique ice cream flavor.

Poppy Seed Ice Cream

Ingredients: Poppy seeds, cream, sugar, milk, vanilla extract.

Preparation: Blend poppy seeds into a creamy mixture, churn, and freeze.

Calories: 220 per serving

Poppy Seed Ice Cream offers a delightful crunch and a subtle nutty flavor.

About Us

Our story begins in a charming little town where two ice cream enthusiasts, Ann and Ben , crossed paths on a sunny summer afternoon. Both had a deep love for the creamy, sweet treat that is ice cream, but neither could have anticipated how this shared passion would shape their lives.

Ben was a talented pastry chef with a flair for creating unique desserts, while Ann was a passionate food critic who had a soft spot for indulgent treats. Their first meeting was at a local ice cream festival, where Ben was showcasing her latest creation—Lavender Honey Ice Cream. Ann , always on the lookout for the perfect scoop, was captivated by the delicate blend of flavors.

Their connection was instant. As they chatted about their favorite flavors and secret recipes, it became clear that they shared not only a love for ice cream but also a vision for bringing joy to others through their passion. Over the years, their friendship blossomed into a deep and enduring love, and together, they dreamed of sharing their ice cream creations with the world.

In 2024, Ann and Ben took a leap of faith and opened i-scream, a haven for ice cream lovers. Their goal was simple: to create a space where every scoop tells a story and every flavor evokes a memory. From the classic Pink Cherry Ice Cream to the innovative Poppy Seed Ice Cream, each flavor is crafted with love and a touch of nostalgia.

At i-scream, every bite is a celebration of their journey—one that began with a simple love for ice cream and grew into a shared dream. They invite you to join them on this sweet adventure and savor the flavors that have become a testament to their love and creativity.
